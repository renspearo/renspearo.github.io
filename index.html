About

"If you can't find it, make it yourself."

The concept of "Renspearo" came about circa 2015 when I decided to take up the sport of spearfishing and freediving after reading articles of awesome stories and then binging on spearfishing videos posted up by some of the veterans in the community. A couple months after making the decision to try it out, I joined a popular online forum where I met my first dive buddies who agreed to take me out on my first shoredive. After doing enough online research, I decided that the best way that I should begin spearfishing was with a polespear, so I went to the nearest sporting goods shop and took a look at their selection of shiny fiberglass polespears. When I saw the price of the spears for how little thought went into engineering them before production, it made me wonder if I could make spear that was cheaper and higher quality. Fast forward a year and I found myself addicted to the sport and meeting the most genuine craftsmen. I'd like to give a special thanks to John Martin, Aaron Crist, and Phil Herranen for being great mentors.
